    ONE CARD READING

    Take the time to centre yourself. Take a few deep breaths. When a clear question arises, 'shuffle' the deck, and then 'draw' a card to reveal the insight you desire.

    Trust your intuition, seeker! Be open, and willing to accept that you may not recieve the outcome you had hoped for.

    Rest assured; whatever is meant to be WILL be...
